About Us

Swatantryaveer Savarkar Mandal is dedicated to the legacy of Veer Savarkar and aims to promote his vision of a self-reliant and empowered society through various social and cultural projects.

Our Projects

Project 1: Education for All

Providing educational resources and support to underprivileged children.

Project 2: Health and Wellness

Organizing health camps and wellness programs in rural areas.

Project 3: Women's Empowerment

Initiatives aimed at empowering women through skill development and self-employment opportunities.

Project 4: Environmental Conservation

Efforts towards preserving the environment and promoting sustainable living.
